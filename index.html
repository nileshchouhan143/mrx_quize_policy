Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

Welcome to MRX Quize. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application. By using this app, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data: We are committed to your privacy. We do NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your Email Address, Phone Number, or Physical Location. Our app does not require any Login or Account creation.

Local Name Input (Share to Story & Certificates): For features like "Share to Story" and generating "Achievement Certificates", the app may ask you to input your name. This name is kept strictly local on your device. It is used solely to generate the image or text for you to use and is never uploaded, stored, or processed on any external server.

Virtual Currency: The "Spin Wheel" and Quiz rewards provide virtual coins. These coins are used strictly for entertainment to unlock levels within the app. These coins have NO real-world monetary value, cannot be withdrawn, and cannot be exchanged for real currency.

2. App Features & Entertainment Disclaimer

Exam Preparation Section: The app includes an Exam Preparation section designed to help users practice for various competitive exams, including but not limited to: SSC GD, Railway Group D, Police Constable, Army GD, and Patwari. This section features two distinct practice formats:

  • Quick Practice: Designed for rapid, on-the-go quiz sessions.
  • Full Mock Test: Designed to simulate a full-length quiz practice environment.
Disclaimer: This section and its tests are created strictly for **entertainment and mock practice purposes only**. This is NOT an official exam preparation tool, nor is it affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any government agency or official exam conducting board.

Achievement Certificates: The app features a gamified achievement system. Upon completing certain milestones, users are awarded an "Achievement Certificate". These certificates are purely virtual, unofficial, and intended solely for entertainment, fun, and showing off progress to friends. They hold no legal, academic, or real-world value.

3. Third-Party Service Providers & Ads

To support the development, monitor app performance, and provide rewards (Double Coins/Unlock Levels), we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device (via Advertising IDs or diagnostic data).

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Rewarded & Banner Ads)
  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Feedback)

AdMob may use your Google Advertising ID to show personalized ads. You can reset or opt-out of this in your device settings under Google > Ads.

We use Google Firebase for diagnostic purposes and user feedback. Feedback data is only sent to and stored on Firebase when the user explicitly submits it within the app. We also analyze general app usage time to help us understand performance and improve the user experience. No personal identifying data is transmitted during this process.

4. Data Storage (Local Only)

We use an on-device database (Room DB and SharedPreferences) to save your quiz progress, achievement status, high scores, and unlocked levels. This data stays 100% on your device and is never uploaded to any external server or shared with third parties.

5. Data Deletion Rights

Since we do not store your data on any server, you have full control over your data. You can delete all your progress, certificates, locally inputted names, and coins by "Clearing App Data" in your device's application settings or by uninstalling the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed towards children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
